# Privacy Policy for ExGo

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

ExGo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

ExGo is designed with privacy in mind. All your financial data is stored **locally on your device** using secure local storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your financial information on our servers.

The following data is stored locally on your device:

- **Transaction Data**: Expenses, income, and savings transactions you create
- **Settings**: Currency preferences, monthly income, theme preferences
- **Categories**: Custom transaction categories you create

### Error Tracking

We use Sentry (a third-party error tracking service) to help us identify and fix bugs. When an error occurs, we may send:

- Error messages and stack traces
- Device information (device model, OS version)
- App version information
- Technical diagnostic data

**We do NOT send any financial data, transaction details, or personal information to Sentry.**

## How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally:

- **We do not have access to your data**
- **We do not analyze your spending patterns**
- **We do not share your data with third parties**
- **We do not use your data for advertising**

Error tracking data is used solely for:
- Identifying and fixing bugs
- Improving app stability
- Technical diagnostics

## Data Security

### Local Storage

- All data is encrypted at rest using your device's built-in security features
- Data is stored in secure local storage (AsyncStorage) on your device
- We recommend enabling biometric authentication or PIN protection in the app settings

### Authentication

- You can enable biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, Fingerprint) or PIN code protection
- Authentication is handled locally on your device
- We do not store or transmit authentication credentials

## Third-Party Services

### Sentry

We use Sentry for error tracking. Sentry's privacy policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

Sentry may collect:
- Error logs and stack traces
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App version
- Technical diagnostic data

**No financial or personal data is sent to Sentry.**

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- **You have full control** over your data
- **You can delete all data** at any time using the "Reset all data" option in Settings
- **You can export your data** using the PDF export feature
- **You can uninstall the app** to remove all data

## Data Retention

- Data is stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
- We do not retain any copies of your data
- Error logs in Sentry are retained according to Sentry's retention policy (typically 90 days)

## Children's Privacy

ExGo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
